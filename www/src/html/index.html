Raildale is an exciting railway building and development game. The rules of the game are rather simple: the gamer connects multi-color cities with railways. There are many futures like new cities, switches and traffic lights, fines and rewards. The gamer has to prevent train crashes and go to same color cities. In case of train crash there are big fines to fix it. Gamers win the level in case of positive balance within a specific time frame. Think it is easy?
